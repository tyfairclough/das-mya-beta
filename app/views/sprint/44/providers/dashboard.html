Getting started

The employer must do the following in their account before any cohorts or apprentices can appear in your account:

  • add their PAYE schemes - so levy funds can credit their account
  • add their organisations and sign agreements for each of these organisations - so they can spend their levy funds
  • start the ‘add an apprentice’ process - so they can authorise payments to you

The register to manage your apprenticeship funds guide contains more information about the steps employers need to follow.
