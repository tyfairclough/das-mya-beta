Getting started

If you’ve agreed to train an employer’s apprentices, the employer must follow these steps before you’ll see any cohorts or apprentices in your account:

  • sign their agreement with the ESFA
  • add PAYE schemes to their account
  • add organisations employing apprentices
  • start the ‘add an apprentice’ process

The apprenticeship funding: how it will work guide contains more information about the overall steps employers need to follow.
