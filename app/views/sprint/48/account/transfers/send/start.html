Offer a monthly transfer

Send government funds from another employer to cover the entire cost of apprenticeship training.

Before you start

You will need:

  • the receiving employer's apprenticeship account ID
  • basic details of the apprenticeships you want to fund:
    • apprenticeship standard/Framework
    • start date
    • number of apprentices
  • available funds to transfer
Start

What you need to know

  • you can't get these funds back once they are sent.
  • you must fund the full cost of the apprenticeship rather than part of it
  • funds may not cover the entire apprenticeship you wish to fund
  • you have forecasted your monthly outgoing payments carefully, so that the receiving employer will get the funds
  • your oldest funds will be used first
  • that the expiry dates of the funds you're transferring will be carried over to the receiving employer
