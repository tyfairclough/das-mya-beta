Autumn

Tasks

Employers will have the capability to see any outstanding tasks in their account to ensure they are able to have the account into a manageable state. E.g. 4 apprentices to be approved, 2 agreements to sign.

Activities

Employers will be presented with an audit trail to enable to them to have complete visibility on all activities within the account.

Search an apprentice by name

Employers and providers will be able to search by apprentice name in manage apprenticeships.

Changes in academic year

Certain restrictions will be in place within the service to cater for the changes to the new academic year.

Display start and end date actuals

Employers have greater visibility of learner start and end dates to help them budget with greater granularity.

Display an EPA

Employers will have the ability to see the End Point Assessor within the service so that their apprentice it qualified.

Funding cap warning messages

Users will be aware of any course price which is above the funding band threshold.

Winter

Forecasting

The employer will have the ability to forecast based on actual payments within their account to help with budgeting and management of levy funds.

Sunsetting

We’ll be notifying employers when their expected funds will expire.

Download transactions

Employers will be able to download transactions they see on the screen which will let them insert the data in their own budgeting/forecasting tools.

Automated stop process

Removing the need for manual intervention when moving a learner from one course to another.

Notice board of change within the service

Notify users of new features within the service in order to best utilise their levy account.

Spring

Archive Apprentices

The employer will have the ability to archive any learner who has completed or stopped their apprenticeship.

View government incentive payment for apprentices

Employers will be able to see what government incentive payments have occurred for a particular employer.

Employer bulk upload

Employers will be able to add multiple apprentices in one go rather than individually.

Improved journey’s for employer/providers

Employers who are employer/providers will be able to use one service in order manage levy account.

Summer

Read only access to view learner details

Users with read only access will be able to view apprentice details within the service.

Increase access levels

Employers have asked for additional roles within their account so that they can give a wider range of access and permissions to their staff.

Sub-accounts

Employers will have the ability to manage their levy funds between connected organisations within their account and add greater restrictions on which data can be displayed between accounts.

Bulk changes for apprentices

The provider will be able to submit changes for learners through a bulk change process.

Non-levy employers

The service will aim to cater for non levy employers too, which will give all employers ownership of the standards and apprenticeships.
