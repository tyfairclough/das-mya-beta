Request a monthly transfer

Receive levy funds from another employer to cover up to the entire cost of apprenticeship training.

Before you start

You will need:

  • the sending employers apprenticeship account ID

Either

  • basic details of the apprenticeships you want to fund:
    • Apprenticeship standard/Framework
    • start date
    • number of apprentices

Or:

  • A monthly transfer amount and duration agreed with the sending employer
Start

What you need to know

  • you can't re-transfer these funds
  • you can't refund unspent funds
  • you can only fund new starts
  • funds you receive will have expiry dates carried over from the sending employers account.
  • no transfers out are permitted until all transfers in are spent
