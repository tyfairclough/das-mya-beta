How this calculation is made

Transferred levy vouchers have their expiry date reset to 24 months from the point of transfer. This means courses over 24 months can not be funded in a single transfer. Further more any lag between the transfer date and the actual start date will reduce the amount the receiving employer can spend before the funds expire.

The calculation works out how much of a course can be funded, taking into account the completion payment representing 20% of the total cost in the final month. Transfers that do not cover the entire cost of the apprenticeship will not include a provision for the final completition payment.

The first calculation based:

  • The cost of the whole apprenticeship
  • minus the 20% completition incentive
  • Which is divded into monthly payments
  • The funds used for the apprenticeship take into account the difference between the transfer date and the start date.
