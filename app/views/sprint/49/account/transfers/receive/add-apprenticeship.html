What you need to know about transfers:
  • If the apprenticeship lasts for longer than 24 months you won’t be able to fund it from a single transfer
  • you need to make sure that the first monthly payment coincides with the start date of the apprenticeship otherwise you could lose that month’s, and subsequeny, funding

The transfer funds show the completion payment of 20% of the total cost of the apprenticeship, which is taken out in the final month.

Transferred funds that do not cover the entire cost of the apprenticeship will not include the final completion payment.
