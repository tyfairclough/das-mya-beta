Offer a monthly transfer

Receive levy funds from another employer to cover up to the entire cost of apprenticeship training.

Before you start

You will need:

  • The receiving employers apprenticeship account ID
  • Basic details of the apprenticeships you want to fund:
    • Apprenticeship standard/Framework
    • Start date
    • Number of apprentices
  • Available funds to transfer
Start

What you need to know

  • You can't get these funds back once they are sent.
  • You can only fund new starts
  • Funds may not cover the entire apprenticeship you wish to fund
  • Insure you have carefully forecasted your outgoing payments to ensure you're commitment to the receiving employer can be honoured.
  • Your oldest funds will be used first
  • The expiry dates of those funds will be carried over to the receiving employer.
