What you need to know about transfers:
  • you have 24 months from the point at which funds are transferred to you to use them
  • If the apprenticeship lasts for longer than 24 months you won’t be able to fund it from a single transfer
  • If the apprenticeship start date is after the transfer has been deposited into your account, you will have less to spend before the funds expire

The transfer funds show the completion payment of 20% of the total cost of the apprenticeship, which is taken out in the final month.

Transferred funds that do not cover the entire cost of the apprenticeship will not include the final completion payment.

Calculation

((27000−(27000×0.2))(48−1) )×(24−2) =35200.0

Important:

As soon as the transferred funds are in your account, the 24 month expiry period will start. If you have received the funds earlier than the course start date or the apprenticeship is more than 24 months, your apprenticeship may not be fully funded.
